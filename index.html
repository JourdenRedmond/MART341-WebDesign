Banana Bread

"Ingredients"

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

"Directions"

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.Lightly grease 9x5 loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour,baking soda and salt
  3. In seperate bowl, cream butter and brown sugar together.
  4. Stir in Eggs and mashed bananas
  5. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture
  6. Pour batter into pan
  7. Bake for 60-65 Min
  8. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes
