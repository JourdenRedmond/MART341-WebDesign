Ingredients

  • 1 cup cold unsalted butter cut into small cubes
  • 3/4 cup lightly packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup cake flour
  • 1 3/4 cup all pourpose flour
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp table salt
  • 2 cups semi sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

Preheat oven to 410 degrees and line two baking sheets.

In a large bowl add butter and sugar and cream for 2 minutes

Add in the eggs

Add in all flour, cornstarch,baking soda, and salt.

Add in chocolate chips and mix

Bake for 9 minutes and then you are done!
