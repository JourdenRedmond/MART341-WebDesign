The parents of Jacob Blake held a press conference Tuesday to speak about their son and the ongoing unrest in Kenosha, Wisconsin. Jacob Blake Jr. Was shot multiple times by a Kenosha, Wisconsin, police officer last Sunday evening. Protesters in Kenosha and elsewhere have demonstrated, demanding police accountability. Some of those protests have turned violent. Jacobs family was emotional as they spoke about their son. His father Jacob Blake Sr’s voice was filled with emotion “They shot my son seven times, seven times like he didn’t matter, but my son matters.” he said. The family also spoke about the recent violent protests that have erupted in Wisconsin and around the country. “As I was riding through the city, I noticed a lot of damage. It does not reflect my son or my family.” said Blakes mother Julia Jackson. With her son fighting for his life in the hospital Jackson had one thing to ask of the country and Wisconsin. “So, I'm really asking everyone and encouraging everyone in Wisconsin and abroad to take a moment and examine your hearts. Citizens, police, firemen, clergy, politicians do Jacob justice on this level and examen your hearts. We need healing.”
